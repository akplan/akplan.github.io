Noteworthy Projects

Fluffy, your Caring Cabbit

Pebble Watch app written in Pebble.js! Fluffy's here to relieve your anxiety any way it possibly can! Essentially a tamagotchi that takes care of you (and doesn't die in its own filth if you lose it between the couch cushions.) Won "Best Wearable Hack" at HackRU, Spring 2015!

Color With Friends

Web app written in HTML/CSS for the LeapMotion!

Color-aborative drawing fun for kids far and near. No muss, no fuss, just good ol' fingerpainting without the mess. Great for children's hospitals. Guest starring Thomas Kang as "Coloring Page 2." Produced by Dick Wolf.

PStock_Watchface

Watchface for the Pebble!

PStock_Watchface mesures how well your stock is doing, according to a cute emoji scale we made ourselves. Ideal for the young professional, as well as the young-at-heart professional. How do you do, fellow kids?

Am I Left Shark?
